About Caerdydd Ffynnon Taf

Caerdydd Ffynnon Taf covers northern and eastern parts of Cardiff, including Llanishen, Cyncoed, Pentwyn, Lisvane, and Pontprennau. This is a predominantly suburban area with high levels of affluence and educational attainment. It includes many of the capital’s commuter neighbourhoods and some of its fastest-growing developments.

Key Stats

  • Caerdydd Ffynnon Taf brings together Cardiff North and Cardiff East.
